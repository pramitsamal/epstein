Leisure and business travelers feel like locals at this historic boutique Now Live
hotel set among the embassies and grand residences of Budapest's
tree-lined Andrassy Avenue. Built in 1937 and completely renovated
three years ago, the hotel is home to a hip-again Bauhaus style in the
comfortable rooms and gourmet dining at on-site Baraka Restaurant.
For exploring, you can't beat the location: It's a short walk to restaurants,
parks, museums and the city's famed thermal baths.
