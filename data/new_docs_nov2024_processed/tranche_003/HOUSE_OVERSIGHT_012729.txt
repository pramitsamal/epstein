The Cervo may have a Swiss address, but there's nothing neutral Now Live
about this boutique hotel's atmosphere and hospitality. In Zermatt, a
legendary ski village where the Matterhorn (Horu to locals) dominates
the landscape (and the activities, yearround) the hotel has the requisite
majestic views. But modern interiors and quirky accents like antlers and
cheerful checkerboard patterns make the Cervo a study in Swiss chic.
