This amazing photo, taken in Alba Iulia, Romania back in 1987, shows construction workers moving a whole house after
it was deemed to be in the way of a new boulevard. The building was split into two parts and moved 55 meters to the side.

The workers moved the 7,600-ton building in just six hours.
