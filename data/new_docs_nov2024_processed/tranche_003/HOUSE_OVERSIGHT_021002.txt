Optional Beneficiaries & Services

Examples of Optional Beneficiaries

e Disabled and elderly with annual
income between $7,082
(Supplementary Security Income, or
SSI) and $9,310 (Federal Poverty
Level, or FPL)

e Nursing home residents with annual
income between $7,082 (SSI) and
$21,000 (3x SSI)

e Pregnant women with annual income
above $12,382 (>133% of FPL)

e Children under 6 with annual family
income above $20,841

(@E) www.kpcb.com

Dental services

durable medical equipment

Hospice services

Inpatient psychiatric hospital
services for individuals under age 21

e Other specialist medical or remedial

care
