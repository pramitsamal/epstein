rush of reconfiguring transcendent experience, some glimmering of grace no matter
how fleeting, an experience of intoxication with God, Martin Buber’s self

moments of illumination, must be attending church or temple to negotiate a better
now and hereafter. Attending synagogue or church without the promise of a

safety and a sharing of propitious contacts for social and economic advantage. Why
