Olivier Sibony is a professor, writer, and advisor specializing in the quality of strategic
thinking and the design of decision processes. Sibony teaches Strategy, Decision Making,
and Problem Solving at HEC Paris. He is also an Associate Fellow of Said Business School
in Oxford University. Previously, he was a Senior Partner with McKinsey & Company,
where he spent 25 years.

Cass R. Sunstein is currently the Robert Walmsley University Professor at Harvard. From
2009 to 2012 he was Administrator of the White House Office of Information and
Regulatory Affairs. From 2013 to 2014, he served on President Obama’s Review Group
on Intelligence and Communication Technologies. From 2016 to 2017, he served on the
Defense Innovation Board of the US Department of Defense. He is the author of the
bestsellers NUDGE (with Richard H. Thaler) and THE WORLD ACCORDING TO STAR
WARS. His two most recent books, IMPEACHMENT: A CITIZEN’S GUIDE and CAN IT
HAPPEN HERE? Are reviewed together on the front page of THE NEW YORK TIMES BOOK
REVIEW (March 18, 2018). Sunstein is the recipient of the 2018 Holberg prize, awarded
annually to a scholar who has made outstanding contributions to research in the arts,
humanities, the social sciences, law or theology.
