Just outside Heraklion, Crete's capital city, the Candia Maris is a Now Live
sprawling beachside resort spanning nearly 10 acres. Although the
property lacks the charm of smaller hotels, it more than compensates
with its amenities, which include tennis courts and seven pools. The
highlight is the vast thalassotherapy spa, which underwent a
multimillion-dollar renovation in 2008.
