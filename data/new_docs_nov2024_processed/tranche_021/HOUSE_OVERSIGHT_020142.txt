ABOUT THE AUTHOR

MICHAEL WOLFF has received numerous awards for his work, including two National
Magazine Awards. He has been a regular columnist for Vanity Fair, New York, The
Hollywood Reporter, British GQ, USA Today, and The Guardian. He is the author of six
prior books, including the bestselling Burn Rate and The Man Who Owns the News. He
lives in Manhattan and has four children.
