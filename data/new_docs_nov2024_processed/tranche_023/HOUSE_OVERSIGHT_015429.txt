drafted. In the army, he was assigned to the 82 Airborne Division at Fort
Bragg in North Carolina. He was a “leg,” though. Instead of jumping out
of an airplane, his job was to maintain all radios, phones and electrical
equipment. He also started the U.S. Helicopter Square Dance Team to
demonstrate the mobility of helicopters. When assigned KP (Kitchen
Police), rather than peel potatoes, he scheduled helicopter square dance
practice.
